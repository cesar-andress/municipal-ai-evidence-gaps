# Ethics and privacy

## Data source scope

This project uses **public documentation** only, retrieved from official municipal websites and publicly linked governance materials.

No freedom-of-information requests, administrative records, internal emails, procurement contracts (unless publicly posted), or personal data extracts are in scope for the initial design.

## Privacy considerations

Public AI governance documents may occasionally reference systems that process personal data. Coders should:

- record governance categories and short locators only
- avoid copying large excerpts of personal or sensitive operational detail
- redact incidental personal identifiers if encountered in excerpts

## Web collection

Automated retrieval must respect `robots.txt`, reasonable request rates, and municipal terms of use. Collection scripts are designed for low-frequency, manifest-driven access rather than bulk scraping.

## Positioning

Documentary evidence gaps are analytic findings about **Public-Document Observability**, not accusations of misconduct, legal compliance determination, or inference of internal governance quality.

## Archival release

Materials deposited to Zenodo will include coded summary data and methodology documentation. Raw HTML snapshots or PDFs will be included only where licensing and municipal publication terms permit.
